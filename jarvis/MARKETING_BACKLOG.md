## Launch Week

- [ ] Post launch announcement across all active channels
- [ ] Reply to every comment and DM in the first 48 hours

## Post-Launch

- [ ] Monitor App Store reviews — respond to all 1–3 star reviews within 24 hours
- [ ] Set up a regular content cadence (weekly tips, user features, update announcements)
- [ ] Track installs, retention, and ratings weekly
- [ ] Consider a referral or share mechanic within the app
- [ ] Plan v1.1 announcement content once first update ships
